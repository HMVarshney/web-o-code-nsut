Booster Figures

Booster is a wooden collectible figure made from ash wood and cast iron. Booster is created by Polygon with a lot of love and passion. He is a joyful, chubby and friendly character. He can pose at your desk or a shelf and remind you of the joy and happiness around you.


He is currently busy making a family with his partner – expect him with a dog and a couple of babies soon. ​

Unistool

The three-legged Unistool is designed to be used in the living room, kitchen, dining room or even in an office or a studio. It’s simple and carved as one-piece without any hardware. Its unique style blends with almost any interior style.

Bench

From designer Miheer Fyzee, this bench is a simple yet elegant artistic bespoke piece of furniture handcrafted out of teak wood with Sycamore Inlay. It has a personality of its own – constructed using no hardware at all, making it not only a truly beautiful piece of furniture, but a feat of craftsmanship and prestigious furniture you will ever own.

Nu lamp

nu lamp is crafted from solid Panda marble with black and white veins. A subtle diffused glow is produced around the lower section when lit. Marble being a natural stone, the hue and veining varies from piece to piece, making each piece unique.

Wave Chair

The wave chair is an artistic designer furniture offering a relaxing posture ideal for poolside leisure or a library setting for those quite moments.

Trio Chair

An excellent fusion of solid wood and rusty metal finish giving a fabulous industrial character to this bar stool. The round seat is designed with a slight indent to provide excellent comfort and the sturdy metal frame brings ultimate stability. It’s perfect for your modern kitchen counter, bar or a favourite corner of your home.
